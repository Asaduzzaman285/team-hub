# Video Walkthrough Script (3-5 Minutes)

### Slot 1: Introduction (30s)
*   **Action:** Start on the Home page (`/`).
*   **Speech:** "Hello, I'm [Your Name]. This is my Collaborative Team Hub. It's a monorepo built with Next.js, Express, and PostgreSQL. Today I'll show you how teams can manage goals and communicate in real-time."
*   **Action:** Click 'Login' and log in with your demo account.

### Slot 2: Workspaces & Presence (45s)
*   **Action:** Land on the Dashboard. Show the workspace cards.
*   **Speech:** "This is the global dashboard where you see all your workspaces. Let's enter the 'Development' workspace."
*   **Action:** Click into a workspace.
*   **Action:** (If possible, open another browser window side-by-side).
*   **Speech:** "Notice the green pulse on my avatar. The app features real-time presence, so you always know who is currently online in your team."

### Slot 3: Goals & Optimistic UI (45s)
*   **Action:** Go to 'Strategic Goals' tab. Add a new goal and click the checkbox.
*   **Speech:** "I've implemented Optimistic UI for goals. When I add a goal or mark it complete, the UI updates instantly before the server even responds, creating a snappy experience."

### Slot 4: Kanban & List View Toggle (45s)
*   **Action:** Switch to the 'Kanban Board' tab. Move an item from 'To Do' to 'In Progress'.
*   **Speech:** "For task management, we have a real-time Kanban board. I also added a List View toggle for users who prefer a high-density, table-style layout for their action items."
*   **Action:** Toggle to 'List View' and back.

### Slot 5: Announcements & @Mentions (45s)
*   **Action:** Go to 'Announcements' tab.
*   **Speech:** "Admins can post rich-text announcements. Team members can react with emojis and comment. I also implemented @mentions—typing a teammate's name in a comment triggers a real-time notification for them."
*   **Action:** Type a comment with `@Demo Admin` and show the notification bell dot.

### Slot 6: Analytics & Audit Log (30s)
*   **Action:** Go to the 'Activity Log' tab then the global 'Analytics' page.
*   **Speech:** "Finally, every action is logged in an immutable Audit Trail. You can also view team performance in the Analytics dashboard, which visualizes velocity and task distribution, and allows for CSV data exports."

### Slot 7: Conclusion (15s)
*   **Action:** Show your name on the profile.
*   **Speech:** "That's the Collaborative Team Hub. Thank you for your time!"
